Getting audio into it

First-time setup

  1. Add the URL as a Browser Source (OBS) / Web Browser input (vMix)
  2. Right-click it → Interact
  3. Click "Enable Audio" once inside that window
  4. Pick your input device from the dropdown that appears

Capturing the actual music

A room mic works, but reacts to everything in the room. For a cleaner reaction to just the music, route your DJ software's output through a virtual audio cable (e.g. VB-CABLE, VoiceMeeter) and pick that cable as the input device instead of your physical mic.
